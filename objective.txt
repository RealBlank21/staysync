Project Scope

Admin (Hostel Management Unit)
- Admin can login into the system.
- Admin can view, approve or reject hostel application.
- Admin can edit, add or remove student entries.

Warden
- Warden can login into the system
- Warden can view student entries

Student
- Students can login into the system.
- Students can fill in the hostel application form.
- Student can view the status of the hostel application.
